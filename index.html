Artist | Jr. Developer


Hello, my name is Sarah LaBarge! I love controlled color and a good pattern clash. My background is in art, display and preservation. Before my coding journey took off, I studied fine arts at Ball State University. With my concentration in drawing, I have worked with a variety of materials to create massive works. My largest drawing is over 8 feet tall. I graduated in May of 2016 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts. During that time I curated local art shows around Muncie, Indiana. Following school, I started working at a local picture framing shop. There I was able to handle all different types of art, old documents and design a proper display for them. As time progressed, I wanted to pursue something more than the tangible. There was something intriguing about coding and creating on a platform that was so abstract to me. Currently, I am in the Eleven Fifty Academy Program. Wrapping my brain around JavaScript has been a rewarding challenge. This journey has taught me a lot about myself and the importance of continued learning. I would love to fuse my creative skills and knowledge of composition with coding in my career after this.
