What's Up?


Hello, my name is Sarah! I love controlled color and a good pattern clash. My background is art, display and preservation. Before my coding journey took off, I studied fine arts at Ball State University. With my concentration in drawing, I have worked with a variety of materials to create massive works. My largest drawing is over 8 feet tall. I graduated in May of 2016 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts. During that time I also curated local shows around Muncie, Indiana. Moving back to Indy, I bounced around different service jobs until I landed a solid job at a local picture framing shop. There I was able to handle all different types of art, old documents and design a proper display for them. As time progressed, I wanted to pursue something more than the tangible. There was something intriguing about coding and creating things on a platform that was so abstract to me. Currently, I am in the Eleven Fifty Academy Program. Wrapping my brain around HTML, CSS and JavaScript has been a rewarding challenge. This journey has taught me a lot about myself and the importance of continuing to educate myself in subject I never thought I could understand. I would love to fuse my creative skills and knowledge of composition with coding in my career after this. Onward and upward from here! To create beautiful websites apps and much more!
